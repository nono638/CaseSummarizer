                            COMPLAINT

        Plaintiff JOHN DOE, by and through his attorneys, respectfully alleges as follows:

PARTIES

        1. At all times relevant herein, plaintiff JOHN DOE was a resident of New York County.

        2. Upon information and belief, defendant ABC HOSPITAL is a hospital corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York, with its principal place of business located at 123 Main Street, New York, New York.

        3. Upon information and belief, defendant DR. JANE SMITH is a physician licensed to practice medicine in the State of New York and was at all relevant times an employee and/or agent of defendant ABC HOSPITAL.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

        4. On or about January 15, 2023, plaintiff presented to the emergency department of ABC HOSPITAL complaining of severe chest pain and shortness of breath.

        5. Plaintiff was examined by defendant DR. JANE SMITH, who negligently failed to order appropriate cardiac testing despite plaintiff's presenting symptoms being consistent with acute coronary syndrome.

        6. As a direct and proximate result of defendants' negligence, plaintiff suffered a myocardial infarction (heart attack) which could have been prevented with timely intervention.

        7. Plaintiff required emergency cardiac catheterization and placement of multiple stents.

        8. Plaintiff has suffered permanent cardiac damage and continues to experience chest pain, fatigue, and limitations on physical activity.

FIRST CAUSE OF ACTION
(Medical Malpractice)

        9. Plaintiff repeats and realleges each and every allegation contained in paragraphs 1 through 8 as if fully set forth herein.

        10. Defendants owed plaintiff a duty to provide medical care in accordance with good and accepted medical practice.

        11. Defendants breached said duty by failing to properly diagnose and treat plaintiff's cardiac emergency.

        12. As a direct and proximate result of defendants' negligence, plaintiff suffered serious and permanent injuries.

WHEREFORE, plaintiff demands judgment against defendants as follows:

        (a) Compensatory damages in an amount to be determined at trial;
        (b) Costs and disbursements of this action;
        (c) Such other and further relief as this Court deems just and proper.
